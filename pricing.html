Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started?

Simply sign up with your university email address and start exploring! You can immediately access all features and begin contributing to the community.

Is my information secure?

Yes! We take privacy seriously. All data is encrypted and we never share your personal information with third parties without your consent.

Is there a mobile app?

Our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices. We're also working on dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android.

How can I contribute?

You can upload event information, share dining hall menus, report traffic conditions, and add study room availability. Every contribution helps the community!

Is this available at my university?

We're constantly expanding to new universities. Contact us to request Project Axion at your campus or check if we're already available there.

Need technical support?

Our support team is here to help! Contact us through this form or email us directly at support@projectaxion.com for technical assistance.
